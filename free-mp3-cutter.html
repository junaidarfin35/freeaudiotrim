What Is Audio Cutting?

Audio cutting means removing unwanted parts of a sound file and keeping only the section you need. Instead of editing the whole file, you focus on a specific segment — like trimming silence, cutting intros, or extracting a short clip.

An online MP3 cutter lets you do this instantly without installing software. You can trim MP3 files online directly in your browser and download the finished result in seconds, whether you are cleaning a recording, cutting a ringtone, or preparing a shorter clip for sharing.
