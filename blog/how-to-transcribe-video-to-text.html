How to Transcribe Video to Text Online

By AudioTools Editorial Team | Published April 30, 2026

How to Transcribe Video to Text

To transcribe video to text, upload a supported video file such as MP4 or MOV to a video to text converter that can process spoken content in the browser. The tool extracts the speech, converts it into a transcript, and lets you export that text for notes, editing, subtitles, or reuse.

MP4 to Text and Video File to Text Workflows

MP4 to text is one of the most common video transcription jobs because many creators, teachers, and teams work with MP4 by default. The same general process also works for broader video file to text workflows when your browser supports the format and the spoken audio is clear.

Can I Convert Video to Text for Free?

Yes. For many everyday needs, a browser-based tool can convert video to text for free without the overhead of account setup or uploading sensitive recordings to a third-party server.

Can I Transcribe Large Audio or Video Files?

Large files can often be processed, but the practical limit depends on your device, browser memory, and the length of the recording. If the source video is especially large, it may help to trim or extract the audio first with Extract Audio from Video before sending it into transcription.

Best Next Step
