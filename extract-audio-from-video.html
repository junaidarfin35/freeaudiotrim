Frequently Asked Questions

Will my video be uploaded?

No — the file stays in your browser. Extraction runs entirely in memory using the Web Audio API.

What formats can I extract audio from?

Most browsers support MP4, MOV, and AVI containers with H.264 + AAC audio tracks. Support varies by browser.

What output format do I get?

Audio is exported as WAV — uncompressed PCM audio ready for editing or further conversion.

Is audio quality preserved?

Yes — the original audio track is decoded as PCM and exported without further compression.

How do I cut the sound from a video file?

Upload the video, let the tool extract audio from the embedded sound track, and then download the resulting WAV file for editing or reuse.
