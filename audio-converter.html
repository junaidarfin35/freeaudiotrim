Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert MP3 to WAV online?

Upload your MP3 file, choose WAV as the output format, and run the conversion in your browser. The converted WAV file is ready to download as soon as processing finishes.

Can I convert audio without uploading files?

Yes. This tool processes files locally on your device, so your audio is not uploaded to a remote server during conversion.

Can I extract audio from video files?

Yes. You can upload a video file and convert or extract the audio into formats like MP3, WAV, M4A, or other supported outputs.

Which audio format should I use?

MP3 is usually best for smaller files and broad compatibility. WAV is useful for editing, FLAC is strong for lossless quality, and M4A can be a good balance between size and quality.

Is audio conversion safe?

Yes. Because conversion runs in your browser, your files stay on your device instead of being sent to an external upload service.

Do I need to install any software?

No. The converter works entirely in your browser, so you can convert files without downloading desktop software or setting up an account.
